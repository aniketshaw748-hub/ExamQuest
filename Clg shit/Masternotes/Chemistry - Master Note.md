# CHAPTER 1 вҖ” Atomic & Molecular Structure

## Core theory (plain language)
Classical mechanics fails for tiny particles (electrons). Four experiments forced "quantum" thinking: **black-body radiation, photoelectric effect, atomic spectra, dual nature of matter**. Out of this came de Broglie's wave-particle duality, Heisenberg's uncertainty, and SchrГ¶dinger's wave equation. Molecular Orbital (MO) theory then explains bonding, stability, bond order and magnetism.

## Key definitions
- **Black body:** a perfect absorber and perfect emitter of all wavelengths.
- **Photoelectric effect:** ejection of electrons from a metal when light of frequency вүҘ threshold ОҪвӮҖ strikes it. Work function WвӮҖ = hОҪвӮҖ.
- **Threshold frequency (ОҪвӮҖ):** minimum frequency that just ejects an electron.
- **Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle:** О”xВ·О”p вүҘ h/4ПҖ вҖ” position and momentum cannot both be known exactly.
- **Bond order:** ВҪ(N_b вҲ’ N_a) where N_b, N_a = electrons in bonding / antibonding MOs.
- **Diamagnetic:** all electrons paired. **Paramagnetic:** вүҘ1 unpaired electron.
- **CFSE:** the net lowering of d-orbital energy when ligands split them.

## Important formulas & derivations (Group-C bread-and-butter)

### (A) de Broglie equation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…
Planck: E = hОҪ.  Einstein: E = mcВІ.  Equate: hОҪ = mcВІ. With ОҪ = c/О»:
$$\boxed{\lambda = \frac{h}{mc} = \frac{h}{mv}}$$
**Numerical drill:** О» = h/(mv). Stone (m=0.1 kg, v=1 m/s) вҶ’ О» вүҲ 6.6Г—10вҒ»ВіВі m (too small вҶ’ no significance). Electron (m=9.1Г—10вҒ»ВіВ№, v=6Г—10вҒө) вҶ’ О» вүҲ 1.2Г—10вҒ»вҒ№ m (measurable). *[WBUT 2018(ODD)]*

### (B) SchrГ¶dinger wave equation вҖ” full derivation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2018(ODD): "Prove (V вҲ’ hВІ/8ПҖВІm вҲҮВІ)ОЁ = EОЁ"]*
Start with a vibrating-string wave: ПҲ = A sin(2ПҖx/О»).
Differentiate twice:  dВІПҲ/dxВІ = вҲ’(4ПҖВІ/О»ВІ)ПҲ.
Put О» = h/mc вҶ’ О»ВІ = hВІ/mВІcВІ:
$$\frac{d^2\psi}{dx^2} = -\frac{4\pi^2 m^2 c^2}{h^2}\psi = -\frac{8\pi^2 m}{h^2}\left(\tfrac{1}{2}mc^2\right)\psi = -\frac{8\pi^2 m}{h^2}(E-V)\psi$$
Extend to 3-D (вҲҮВІ = вҲӮВІ/вҲӮxВІ+вҲӮВІ/вҲӮyВІ+вҲӮВІ/вҲӮzВІ):
$$\nabla^2\psi + \frac{8\pi^2 m}{h^2}(E-V)\psi = 0 \;\;\Rightarrow\;\; \boxed{\left(V-\frac{h^2}{8\pi^2 m}\nabla^2\right)\psi = E\psi}$$

# CHAPTER 2 вҖ” Spectroscopic Techniques & Applications

## Core theory
All spectroscopy = matter interacting with EM radiation. Different regions probe different transitions:

| Technique | Source/region | What transitions | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|---|
| **UV-Vis** | H-lamp(UV)/W-lamp(Vis), 190вҖ“800 nm | valence eвҒ»: ПғвҶ’Пғ*, ПҖвҶ’ПҖ*, nвҶ’ПҖ*, nвҶ’Пғ* | conjugation, vitamins, dissociation const; NOT functional groups |
| **IR** | heated rare-earth rod | bond vibrations (stretch/bend) | functional groups, H-bonding, cis/trans |
| **NMR** | strong magnet, radio freq | nuclear spin flip (Iвү 0) | number & environment of H atoms |
| **Mass** | electron bombardment | fragmentation into ions | molecular weight, formula, structure |

## Key definitions
- **Chromophore:** unsaturated group that absorbs UV/Vis (C=C, C=O, N=NвҖҰ).
- **Auxochrome:** lone-pair group (вҲ’OH, вҲ’NHвӮӮ, вҲ’X) that shifts absorption when attached to a chromophore.
- **Bathochromic (red) shift:** absorption moves to **longer** О». **Hypsochromic (blue):** shorter О».
- **Hyperchromic:** intensity вҶ‘. **Hypochromic:** intensity вҶ“.
- **Fingerprint region:** 1400вҖ“650 cmвҒ»В№ (IR) вҖ” unique molecular ID.
- **IR-active:** vibration must change dipole moment (so symmetric molecules like COвӮӮ stretch, 2,3-dimethyl-2-butene C=C are IR-transparent).
- **NMR-active:** nuclear spin I вү  0 (В№H yes; В№вҒ¶O, В№ВІC no). Internal standard = **TMS**.

## BeerвҖ“Lambert Law вҖ” full derivation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2018(ODD)]*
Lambert: вҲ’dI/dx вҲқ I. Beer: вҲ’dI/dx вҲқ C. Combine:
$$-\frac{dI}{dx} = \alpha_v I C \;\Rightarrow\; -\frac{dI}{I} = \alpha_v C\,dx$$
Integrate IвӮҖвҶ’I over 0вҶ’x:
$$\ln\frac{I_0}{I} = \alpha_v C x \;\Rightarrow\; \log\frac{I_0}{I} = \frac{\alpha_v}{2.303}Cx = \varepsilon C x$$
$$\boxed{A = \log\frac{I_0}{I} = \varepsilon C l}\qquad T=\frac{I}{I_0},\quad A=\log\frac1T$$
**Absorbance is linearly proportional to concentration** вҖ” that is the proof asked for.

# CHAPTER 3 вҖ” Intermolecular Forces & Potential Energy Surfaces

## Core theory
Weak secondary forces between molecules = **van der Waals forces** (dipoleвҖ“dipole, ionвҖ“dipole, dipoleвҖ“induced dipole; H-bonding is a special strong dipoleвҖ“dipole). Real gases deviate from ideal because (1) molecules have finite volume, (2) they attract each other вҖ” most at **high P, low T**.

## Key definitions
- **Hydrogen bond:** weak bond between an electronegative atom and an H already bonded to N/O/F.
- **Ideal gas:** obeys PV=nRT at all T,P (no real gas is ideal).
- **Compressibility factor Z = PV/nRT.** Z=1 ideal; Z>1 less compressible (HвӮӮ, He); Z<1 more compressible.
- **Boyle temperature T_b = a/Rb:** T at which a real gas behaves ideally over a pressure range.
- **Critical temperature T_c:** above it a gas cannot be liquefied by pressure alone.
- **Excluded volume / co-volume b:** = 4Г— actual molecular volume.

## van der Waals equation & critical constants вҖ” derivation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[MODEL but core]*
$$\left(P+\frac{n^2a}{V^2}\right)(V-nb)=nRT$$
- 'a' = intermolecular attraction (high a вҶ’ easy to liquefy: SOвӮӮ, NHвӮғ, COвӮӮ). Unit: atmВ·LВІВ·molвҒ»ВІ.
- 'b' = molecular size correction. Unit: LВ·molвҒ»В№.

**Critical constants from вҲӮP/вҲӮV = 0 and вҲӮВІP/вҲӮVВІ = 0:**
$$\boxed{V_c = 3b}\qquad \boxed{T_c=\frac{8a}{27Rb}}\qquad \boxed{P_c=\frac{a}{27b^2}}$$
**Critical compressibility:** Z_c = P_cV_c/RT_c = **3/8 = 0.375** (same for all vdW gases).

**Derivation skeleton (memorize the 4 steps):**
1. P = nRT/(VвҲ’nb) вҲ’ anВІ/VВІ. Take вҲӮP/вҲӮV and вҲӮВІP/вҲӮVВІ, set both = 0.
2. Combine the two equations вҶ’ 2/(VвҲ’nb) = 3/V вҶ’ **V = 3nb = V_c**.
3. Substitute V_c back into вҲӮP/вҲӮV=0 вҶ’ **T_c = 8a/27Rb**.
4. Put V_c, T_c into vdW eqn вҶ’ **P_c = a/27bВІ**.

## Behaviour of vdW gas at limits (frequent Group-C) вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…
- **Low P:** V large вҶ’ drop a/VВІ and b вҶ’ **PV = RT** (ideal).
- **Moderate P:** keep a/VВІ вҶ’ Z = 1 вҲ’ a/(RTV) (Z<1).
- **High P:** drop a/VВІ keep b вҶ’ Z = 1 + Pb/RT (Z>1).
- **High T:** drop both вҶ’ PV = RT (ideal).
- **HвӮӮ, He:** 'a' negligible вҶ’ Z = 1 + Pb/RT > 1 always (explains why they don't dip below Z=1).

# CHAPTER 4 вҖ” Thermodynamics-I  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… (heaviest derivation chapter)

> **Sign convention used by the book:** q = О”U + W (W = work done **by** system), so О”U = q вҲ’ W.

## Core theory & definitions
- **System types:** Isolated (no matter, no energy), Closed (energy only), Open (both). A living body = open system.
- **Internal energy U:** total inherent energy; state function; (dq)_V = (dU)_V.
- **Enthalpy H = U + PV:** heat content; (dq)_P = (dH)_P.
- **Isothermal:** T constant. **Adiabatic:** q=0. **Isobaric:** P const. **Isochoric:** V const.
- **Reversible:** infinite slow steps, equilibrium throughout, **W is maximum**. **Irreversible:** one step, against constant external P, W less.
- **First law:** q = О”U + W (energy conserved). Limitations: gives no direction, extent, spontaneity, or randomness вҶ’ needs 2nd law.

## THE SIX MUST-KNOW DERIVATIONS

### (1) Cp вҲ’ Cv = R for ideal gas вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2006, 2010E, 2012E]*
General: Cp вҲ’ Cv = T(вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V (вҲӮV/вҲӮT)_P.
Ideal gas PV=RT вҶ’ (вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V = R/V, (вҲӮV/вҲӮT)_P = R/P.
$$C_p-C_v = T\cdot\frac{R}{V}\cdot\frac{R}{P} = R\cdot\frac{RT}{PV} = \boxed{R}$$

### (2) PVОі = constant (adiabatic reversible) вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2006, 2008, 2010E, 2012E, 2016E]*
From TVОівҒ»В№ = const and PV=RT: divide PвӮҒVвӮҒ/PвӮӮVвӮӮ = TвӮҒ/TвӮӮ and substitute вҶ’
$$\boxed{PV^{\gamma} = \text{constant}}\quad(\text{isothermal: }PV=\text{const})$$

### (3) TVОівҒ»В№ = constant вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2011E, 2015E]*
Adiabatic q=0 вҶ’ dU = вҲ’W вҶ’ nCv dT = вҲ’(nRT/V)dV. Integrate:
$$C_v\ln\frac{T_2}{T_1} = -R\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1},\quad (\gamma-1)=\frac{R}{C_v}$$
$$\Rightarrow \boxed{T_1V_1^{\gamma-1} = T_2V_2^{\gamma-1} = \text{const}}$$

### (4) Maximum work in isothermal reversible expansion вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2011E, 2012O, 2014E, 2015O]*
Expand by infinitesimal dP each step; sum the steps:
$$W = \int_{V_1}^{V_2} P\,dV = \int_{V_1}^{V_2}\frac{nRT}{V}dV = \boxed{nRT\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1}=nRT\ln\frac{P_1}{P_2}}$$
**Proof W_rev > W_irrev:** W_rev вҲ’ W_irrev = (RT/PвӮҒPвӮӮ)(PвӮҒвҲ’PвӮӮ)ВІ > 0 always вҮ’ reversible work is maximum.

### (5) Adiabatic work W = (PвӮҒVвӮҒ вҲ’ PвӮӮVвӮӮ)/(ОівҲ’1) вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2017E]* (also W = Cv(TвӮҒвҲ’TвӮӮ))
$$W=\int_{V_1}^{V_2}\frac{K}{V^\gamma}dV = \frac{1}{1-\gamma}(P_2V_2-P_1V_1)=\boxed{\frac{P_1V_1-P_2V_2}{\gamma-1}}=C_v(T_1-T_2)$$

### (6) О”U and О”H via Maxwell relations вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2016E]*
dU = nCv dT + [T(вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V вҲ’ P]dV. For ideal gas the bracket = P вҲ’ P = 0 вҶ’ **dU = nCv dT вҶ’ О”U = nCv(TвӮӮвҲ’TвӮҒ)**.
dH = nCp dT + [вҲ’T(вҲӮV/вҲӮT)_P + V]dP вҶ’ for ideal gas вҶ’ **dH = nCp dT вҶ’ О”H = nCp(TвӮӮвҲ’TвӮҒ)**.
(Uses Maxwell (вҲӮS/вҲӮV)_T=(вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V and (вҲӮS/вҲӮP)_T=вҲ’(вҲӮV/вҲӮT)_P.)

**Adiabatic PвҖ“V slope is ОіГ— steeper than isothermal** (because (dP/dV)_adia = вҲ’ОіP/V vs вҲ’P/V). *[2011E, 2015O]*

## Numericals you must be able to do (frequent)

# CHAPTER 5 вҖ” Thermodynamics-II  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…

## Core theory & 2nd-law statements
- **Clausius:** heat can't flow coldвҶ’hot by itself.
- **Kelvin:** can't get work by cooling a body below the coldest surroundings.
- **Ostwald:** no perpetual-motion machine of the 2nd kind (can't convert heat fully into work).
- **Conclusion:** work obtained only when heat flows hot(source T)вҶ’cold(sink TвҖІ); only a fraction converts.

## Key definitions
- **Entropy S:** dS = dq_rev/T; measure of disorder; state function; (dS)_universe > 0 for spontaneous.
- **Helmholtz free energy A = U вҲ’ TS:** available internal energy; вҲ’(О”A)_T = W_max.
- **Gibbs free energy G = H вҲ’ TS:** available enthalpy; вҲ’(О”G)_T,P = W_non-mechanical (useful work).
- **Chemical potential Ој_i = (вҲӮ(nG)/вҲӮn_i):** partial molar Gibbs energy.

## CORE DERIVATIONS

### (1) Carnot efficiency вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2009, 2011O, 2013E, 2013O, 2016O]*
Four reversible steps (isothermal expand at T, adiabatic expand, isothermal compress at TвҖІ, adiabatic compress). Net work:
$$W = R(T-T')\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1}$$
$$\boxed{\eta=\frac{W}{Q}=\frac{T-T'}{T}=1-\frac{T'}{T}}$$
Since some heat QвҖІ must go to the sink, W<Q вҮ’ **О· < 1** (100% impossible). This is the mathematical form of the 2nd law.

### (2) GibbsвҖ“Helmholtz equation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2006, 2007, 2009, 2011E, 2012E, 2015O, 2016E, 2018O]*
From A=UвҲ’TS, dA=вҲ’PdVвҲ’SdT вҶ’ (вҲӮA/вҲӮT)_V = вҲ’S. Substitute into A=UвҲ’TS:
$$A = U + T\left(\frac{\partial A}{\partial T}\right)_V \qquad\text{and}\qquad \left[\frac{\partial}{\partial T}\left(\frac{A}{T}\right)\right]_V=-\frac{U}{T^2}$$
Similarly from G=HвҲ’TS, (вҲӮG/вҲӮT)_P = вҲ’S:
$$\boxed{G = H + T\left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial T}\right)_P}\qquad \boxed{(\Delta G)_T=\Delta H + T\left[\frac{\partial}{\partial T}(\Delta G)\right]_P}\qquad \left[\frac{\partial}{\partial T}\left(\frac{\Delta G}{T}\right)\right]_P=-\frac{\Delta H}{T^2}$$
**Use:** evaluate О”H from cell EMF: О”H = nF[T(вҲӮE/вҲӮT) вҲ’ E]; derive van't Hoff eqn.

### (3) Entropy of mixing of ideal gases, О”S_mix > 0 вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2018O]*
$$\boxed{\Delta S_{mix} = -R\sum x_i \ln x_i}$$
Since each x_i < 1, ln x_i < 0 вҶ’ О”S_mix is always positive. (О”H_mix = 0; О”G_mix = вҲ’TО”S_mix < 0, spontaneous.)

### (4) Maxwell's relations вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2014O, 2017E]*
| Relation | From |
|---|---|
| (вҲӮP/вҲӮS)_V = вҲ’(вҲӮT/вҲӮV)_S | U |
| (вҲӮV/вҲӮS)_P = (вҲӮT/вҲӮP)_S | H = U+PV |
| **(вҲӮS/вҲӮV)_T = (вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V** | A = UвҲ’TS |
| вҲ’(вҲӮS/вҲӮP)_T = (вҲӮV/вҲӮT)_P | G = HвҲ’TS |
*(Derive (вҲӮS/вҲӮV)_T=(вҲӮP/вҲӮT)_V from A: dA=вҲ’PdVвҲ’SdT, cross-differentiate, dA exact.)*

### (5) Criteria of spontaneity вҶ’ О”G < 0 вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2013E]*

| | Spontaneous | Equilibrium |
|---|---|---|
| (dS)_U,V | > 0 | = 0 |
| (dA)_T,V | < 0 | = 0 |
| **(dG)_T,P** | **< 0** | = 0 |

О”G = О”H вҲ’ TО”S. Reaction spontaneous if О”G < 0. If О”H>0 and О”S>0 вҶ’ spontaneous at **high T** (TО”S > О”H).

## Numericals (entropy & free energy вҖ” common Group B/C)
- **Multi-step entropy** (iceвҶ’steam): О”S = mc ln(TвӮӮ/TвӮҒ) for heating + (mL/T) for phase change. Sum all stages. *[2014O, MODEL]*
- **Entropy of mixing:** О”S = вҲ’R ОЈ x_i ln x_i (per mole). Five-mole A+B (x=0.5 each) вҶ’ О”S=5.76 J/molВ·K; О”G=вҲ’1.73 kJ. *[MODEL]*

- **Two-engine efficiency** (1000вҶ’600вҶ’300 K) totals same 0.7 as single 1000вҶ’300 K. *[MODEL]*

# CHAPTER 6 вҖ” Electrochemical Cell  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳҶ

## Core theory & definitions
- **Galvanic cell:** chemical energy вҶ’ electrical energy (spontaneous redox). **Electrolytic cell:** electrical вҶ’ chemical (non-spontaneous).
- **Anode** = oxidation = (вҲ’). **Cathode** = reduction = (+). (Mnemonic: "**An Ox, Red Cat**".)
- **Cell notation:** anode | anode soln вҖ– cathode soln | cathode. Daniell: Zn | ZnВІвҒә вҖ– CuВІвҒә | Cu.
- **Salt bridge:** inverted U-tube of KCl/KNOвӮғ in agar; maintains electrical neutrality; uses ions of nearly equal transport number (~0.5).
- **Standard electrode potential EВ°:** vs SHE, unit activity, 1 atm, 298 K. **SHE potential = 0 V.**
- **E_cell = (E_red)вӮҠ вҲ’ (E_red)вӮӢ** (cathode minus anode reduction potential). Spontaneous if E_cell > 0.

## Nernst equation вҖ” derivation вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2007, 2014E, 2016O, 2017O]*
For nвӮҒA + nвӮӮB вҮҢ nвӮғD + nвӮ„E: О”G = О”GВ° + RT ln(a_D^nвӮғВ·a_E^nвӮ„ / a_A^nвӮҒВ·a_B^nвӮӮ). With О”G = вҲ’nFE, О”GВ°=вҲ’nFEВ°:
$$\boxed{E = E^\circ - \frac{RT}{nF}\ln\frac{a_D^{n_3}a_E^{n_4}}{a_A^{n_1}a_B^{n_2}} = E^\circ - \frac{0.0591}{n}\log Q}\ (\text{at }25В°C)$$

## EMF вҶ” О”G and О”H вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2018O]*
$$\boxed{\Delta G = -nFE}\qquad \boxed{\Delta H = nF\!\left[T\!\left(\frac{\partial E}{\partial T}\right)_P - E\right]}$$
(вҲӮE/вҲӮT = temperature coefficient of cell.) Also **О”GВ° = вҲ’nFEВ° = вҲ’RT ln K** вҶ’ log K = nFEВ°/2.303RT.

## pH measurement (the recurring Group-C descriptive) вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2006, 2011O, 2012E, 2014O, 2016E, 2017E, 2018O]*
**Hydrogen electrode:** Pt,HвӮӮ(P atm)|HвҒә. Reduction potential
$$E = -\frac{RT}{2F}\ln P_{H_2} - \frac{2.303RT}{F}\text{pH}$$
At P=1 atm, E = вҲ’0.0591В·pH вҶ’ measure E вҶ’ get pH.
**Calomel + Quinhydrone (practical method):** add quinhydrone (Q + HвӮӮQ, equimolar) to test solution with Pt; couple with saturated calomel (E=0.2415 V).
$$\text{pH} = \frac{-E_{cell} + E^\circ_{Q/H_2Q} - E_{calomel}}{0.059}$$
Quinhydrone advantages: simple, quick equilibrium, small volume, no air-exclusion. Limit: fails in alkaline medium (pH>8) and with other redox systems.

## Reference electrodes

# CHAPTER 8 вҖ” Corrosion  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳҶвҳҶ

## Core theory & definitions
- **Corrosion:** natural destruction of metal back to oxide/hydroxide/sulphide (metals revert to lower-energy ore form).
- **Dry corrosion:** direct attack of OвӮӮ / gases (ClвӮӮ, SOвӮӮ) with no moisture. Sub-types: oxidation corrosion, corrosion by gases, liquid-metal corrosion.
- **Wet (electrochemical) corrosion:** in aqueous/acidic medium; separate anodic & cathodic areas. **More common.**
- **PillingвҖ“Bedworth rule:** if oxide volume вүҘ metal volume вҶ’ non-porous, **protective** (AlвӮӮOвӮғ); if oxide volume < metal volume вҶ’ porous, **non-protective** (alkali metal oxides).

## Mechanisms (Group-C / B)
**Electrochemical corrosion (rusting, OвӮӮ-absorption type):**
- Anode: Fe вҶ’ FeВІвҒә + 2eвҒ» (corrosion here).
- Cathode: ВҪOвӮӮ + HвӮӮO + 2eвҒ» вҶ’ 2OHвҒ».
- FeВІвҒә + 2OHвҒ» вҶ’ Fe(OH)вӮӮ вҶ’ further oxidised to rust FeвӮӮOвӮғВ·3HвӮӮO.

**Hydrogen-evolution type (acidic, very dilute acid):**
- Anode: Fe вҶ’ FeВІвҒә + 2eвҒ» ; Cathode: 2HвҒә + 2eвҒ» вҶ’ HвӮӮвҶ‘.

**Caustic embrittlement (stress corrosion in boilers):** NaвӮӮCOвӮғ + HвӮӮO вҶ’ 2NaOH + COвӮӮ; NaOH seeps into stressed cracks; 2NaOH + Fe вҶ’ NaвӮӮFeOвӮӮ + HвӮӮ; stressed iron = anode вҶ’ fails.

## Factors increasing corrosion rate
Low reduction potential metal; large anodeвҖ“cathode potential difference; dust/oil patches (pitting); **low hydrogen over-voltage**; absence of protective film; **low pH** (acidic); high temperature; **small anode + large cathode area** (worst case).

## Galvanic series logic
"Iron corrodes with copper but not zinc" вҖ” the metal **higher** (more active) in the series is the anode. Zn protects Fe (Zn anodic, sacrificial); Cu makes Fe corrode (Fe anodic).

## Tables
**Dry vs Wet corrosion**

| Dry | Wet |
|---|---|
| Direct gas attack, no moisture | Electrochemical, aqueous |
| Less common | More common |
| Vapours/gases | Conducting liquid |
| oxidation / gas / liquid-metal | differential metal/aeration/crevice |

## Common mistakes
- Corrosion occurs at the **anode**; rust deposits where OвӮӮ is available (cathode region).
- PillingвҖ“Bedworth: bigger oxide volume = protective (counter-intuitive).
- Water-line / crevice / pitting are all **differential aeration** (less OвӮӮ region corrodes).

## Memory tricks
- "**Anode dies**" (oxidation, corrosion).
- "**Less OвӮӮ вҶ’ anode**" (differential aeration: covered/deep region corrodes).
- "Zn **saves** Fe; Cu **kills** Fe."

# CHAPTER 9 вҖ” Periodic Properties  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳҶ (biggest MCQ chapter: 59 MCQs)

## Core trends (recite the direction)
- **Atomic radius:** вҶ“ across period (Z_eff вҶ‘), вҶ‘ down group.
- **Ionic radius:** cation < atom < anion. Isoelectronic: more protons вҶ’ smaller (NaвҒә>MgВІвҒә>AlВівҒә; NВівҒ»>OВІвҒ»>FвҒ»).

# CHAPTER 10 вҖ” Stereochemistry  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳҶ

## Core theory & definitions
- **Isomers:** same molecular formula, different arrangement.
- **Structural isomerism:** skeletal, positional, functional-group. **Metamerism:** different alkyl groups around O/N (e.g. CвӮ„HвӮҒвӮҖO ethers). **Tautomerism:** ketoвҮҢenol dynamic equilibrium.
- **Stereoisomerism:** same structure, different 3-D arrangement вҶ’ optical & geometric.
- **Optically active:** rotates plane-polarized light. **d/(+)** = clockwise; **l/(вҲ’)** = anticlockwise.
- **Chiral:** non-superimposable on mirror image. Chirality needs **absence of** plane of symmetry (Пғ), centre of symmetry (i), and alternating axis (SвӮҷ).
- **Enantiomers:** non-superimposable mirror images. **Diastereomers:** stereoisomers that are NOT mirror images. **Meso:** has internal plane of symmetry вҶ’ optically **inactive** despite chiral centres. **Racemic:** 1:1 d+l mixture, inactive.

## Isomer-counting rules (вҳ… guaranteed Group-B/C) вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…
Let n = number of chiral carbons.
- **Unsymmetrical molecule:** optical isomers = **2вҒҝ**, meso = 0.
- **Symmetrical, even n:** optical = **2^(nвҲ’1)**, meso = **2^(n/2 вҲ’ 1)**, total = 2^(nвҲ’1) + 2^(n/2вҲ’1).
- **Symmetrical, odd n:** optical = 2^(nвҲ’1) вҲ’ 2^((nвҲ’1)/2), meso = 2^((nвҲ’1)/2).

| Example | n | Optical | Meso | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2-bromo-3-chlorobutane (unsym) | 2 | 4 | 0 | 4 |
| Tartaric / butane-2,3-diol (sym, even) | 2 | 2 | 1 | 3 |
| 2,3,4-trihydroxyglutaric acid (sym, odd) | 3 | 2 | 2 | 4 |

## R-S (CahnвҖ“IngoldвҖ“Prelog) вҳ…вҳ…
1. Assign priority by atomic number (higher = higher priority): I > Br > Cl > F > O > N > C > H.
2. Tie вҶ’ compare next atoms outward. Double bond вҶ’ duplicate the atom.
3. View with **lowest priority (4) pointing away**; 1вҶ’2вҶ’3 clockwise = **R**, anticlockwise = **S**.

## E-Z system вҳ…вҳ…
Compare priorities on each doubly-bonded carbon. Higher-priority groups on **same side** = **Z** (Zusammen). Opposite sides = **E** (Entgegen). E-Z is unambiguous; cis/trans is not (use E-Z for complex alkenes). Maleic acid = Z; fumaric acid = E.

## erythro / threo
**Erythro:** like groups on the **same** side (Fischer). **Threo:** like groups on **opposite** sides.

## Projection formulas (Fischer / Newman / Sawhorse)
- **Fischer:** vertical bonds go **behind** plane, horizontal bonds come **forward**; chiral C at the cross. Allowed manipulation: rotate 180В° in plane only.
- **Sawhorse:** view the CвҖ“C bond diagonally; shows front/back substituents.
- **Newman:** view down the CвҖ“C axis; front C = dot in circle, back C = circle. **Staggered** (most stable, e.g. anti n-butane) vs **eclipsed** (least stable).

## ASCII: Fischer of meso-tartaric acid
```
        COOH
         |
    H ---+--- OH      вҶҗ horizontal bonds toward you
         |
    H ---+--- OH      вҶҗ internal plane of symmetry (Пғ)
         |            вҮ’ MESO, optically inactive
        COOH
```

## Common mistakes
- Meso has chiral centres but is **optically inactive** (internal mirror plane).
- Counting: use the right formula (symmetry & even/odd n matter).
- In Fischer: horizontals are toward viewer, verticals away (don't flip 90В°).

## Memory tricks
- "**2^(nвҲ’1) for symmetric, 2вҒҝ for not**."
- "**E = Enemies (opposite); Z = Ze same side**."
- "**Staggered is stronger**" (most stable conformation).

# CHAPTER 11 вҖ” Organic Reactions & Synthesis of Drug Molecules  вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…

## Core species & definitions
- **Free radical:** homolytic cleavage, neutral odd-electron (CHвӮғвҖў).
- **Carbocation:** heterolytic cleavage, +ve on C, spВІ, empty p. Stability **3В° > 2В° > 1В°** (alkyl +I, hyperconjugation; benzyl/allyl by resonance; PhвӮғCвҒә most stable).
- **Carbanion:** вҲ’ve on C with lone pair, spВі pyramidal. Stability **methyl > 1В° > 2В° > 3В°** (opposite of carbocation; вҲ’I stabilizes, resonance PhвӮғCвҒ» most stable).
- **Electrophile:** electron-deficient (HвҒә, вҒәCHвӮғ, BFвӮғ, AlClвӮғ, FeClвӮғ). **Nucleophile:** electron-rich (OHвҒ», CNвҒ», NHвӮғ, HвӮӮO; ambident CNвҒ»).

## SN1 vs SN2 вҖ” the headline comparison вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2007, 2009, 2010O, 2014E, 2014O, 2015E, 2016E, 2016O]*

| Substrate | 3В° > benzyl > 2В° (stable cation) | CHвӮғ > 1В° > 2В° (less hindrance) |

Reactivity to SN1: **3В° > benzyl > 2В° > allyl > phenyl > 1В°.** Reactivity to SN2: **CHвӮғ > 1В° > 2В° > 3В°** (n-butyl chloride most reactive).

## Markovnikov & anti-Markovnikov вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2011E, 2012O, 2013E, 2013O]*
- **Markovnikov:** negative part of HX adds to C with **fewer H** (via more stable carbocation). Propene + HBr вҶ’ **2-bromopropane**.
- **Anti-Markovnikov (peroxide effect, only HBr):** radical mechanism вҶ’ Br adds to C with **more H**. Propene + HBr/peroxide вҶ’ **1-bromopropane**. (CFвӮғ-CH=CHвӮӮ gives anti-Markovnikov too because вҲ’CFвӮғ is electron-withdrawing.)
- HOCl, oxymercuration: follow Markovnikov (OH to fewer-H carbon).

## Aromatic electrophilic substitution вҳ…вҳ…вҳ… *[WBUT 2010E, 2014O, 2015O, 2016E, 2017O]*

- **Nitration:** HNOвӮғ + 2HвӮӮSOвӮ„ вҶ’ NOвӮӮвҒә + HвӮғOвҒә + 2HSOвӮ„вҒ»; benzene + NOвӮӮвҒә вҶ’ nitrobenzene.
